Why should you choose us?

Al-Waleed is a global leader in renewable energy and sustainable development. Over the past decade, we have established a global protocol for the production, export and use of green hydrogen, creating commercially viable solutions in clean energy, sustainable real estate and clean technology in every country in the world.

Headquartered in Jordan in Zarqa Governorate, we develop renewable energy projects, achieve low carbon urban development and promote clean technology innovation. We spread awareness about the global climate crisis, and provide global knowledge and industry platforms.

AtAl-Waleed, we support the world's transition to green energy. We aim to be a role model in the industry of energy and environment solutions and to reduce the carbon footprint at a low cost.

wind

The Aqaba Wind Power Plant is the first operating wind power plant on a commercial scale in the Middle East. The station, located in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, with a capacity of 117 megawatts, contributed to an increase in the total capacity of generating electricity in Jordan by 3%. This project, which is estimated to cost $287 million, was put into operation in September 2015, and was officially inaugurated in the same year in the presence of the Jordanian monarch, King Abdullah. renewable energy project

solar

The Zarqa solar photovoltaic power plant project in the Hashemite area is one of the renewable energy projects of the Ministry of Energy, and the first projects in Zarqa Governorate for renewable energy. The station uses solar energy through photovoltaic (PV) technology to generate electricity. , the energy it will produce will be purchased through an agreement with the Jordan Energy Purchase Company for a period of up to 25 years. The station consists of 2.2 million solar panels, built on an area of ​​6.6 km2, and its location was carefully selected and secured by a specialized Jordanian technical team, in order to achieve the highest possible quality of electric energy production. The project provided a world record tariff in the solar PV sector of 1.2 cents/kWh.

green hydrogen

Expanding green hydrogen projects to reach “zero carbon” Green hydrogen is an environmentally compatible chemical energy storage material In 2021, the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) issued warnings about the urgent need to shift from dependence on polluting fossil fuels as a source of energy to other energy sources free of air polluting emissions, to stave off the effects of the worst climate crisis the world may witness in an era post-industrial. Among the solutions proposed to achieve this goal, green hydrogen was one of the potential solutions for use in the most energy-intensive sectors, such as the aviation and marine shipping sectors. Therefore, we launched a hydrogen production project in South Africa, which is still being worked on
